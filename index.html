OUR STORY

a trip down memory lane

Ahh-Yum was born out of a longing to have home-cooked comfort food whenever and wherever.

The warm aroma of galangal swimming in a pool of santan, the tenderness of a single soft-poached egg resting in the midst of fragrant steamed rice, and fresh cut coriander resting regally on top sealing in all the goodness beneath it. We’ve been on a long search for something that would merge our traditional tastebuds with the modern lifestyles we were leading. Something on-the-go, takes little to no hassle to obtain, but with all the innate goodness and quality of home-cooked food ...

HOW TO PRONOUNCE

—Ahh-Yum—

[noun] [exclamation]

Pronounced: ‘Ayam’; meaning ‘chicken’; poultry; a domestic fowl;

A meat loved by all; also an expression used to describe one’s gastronomic enjoyment.

Example: Ahh, yummm!
